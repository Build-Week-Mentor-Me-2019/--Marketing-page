Helping Entrepreneurs Daily

Mentor Me lets you lend your business skills to entrepreneurs across the globe. Less experienced entrepreneurs often have a lot of questions and do not have a mentor to ask. As an entrepreneur, whenever you need business advice, our volunteers are happy to help. By posting your questions, you can communicate directly and solve a problem. As an experienced business owner you can help pay it forward just by installing the Mentor Me app.

Decision Making Advice

  • Business Growth

    Newer entrepreneurs often find themselves in need of assistance even when their business is doing great. In many occasions, a business is succeeding but has issues with how ton handle that success, invest, and grow in a way that protects the future of the business. There have been many cases where decisions made early on have severely damaged or even killed a company. This is why reaching out to a mentor to learn how to successfully grow your business is vital to your future.

  • Network of Mentors

    Mentor Me has an exclusive network of experienced business people from all industries and major business centers in the world. We guarantee that we have a suitable mentor for you with helpful knowledge in your industry. And this isn't just about your business; Mentor Me extends to helping you live a fulfilling and fruitful personal life as well. Our mentors have been in the tough spots you are facing yourself right now; let us help! Ask an experienced entrepreneur for advice now!

  • Easy to use as a Mentor and an Entrepreneur

    Mentor Me is an easy to use application for both a Mentor looking to share his or her expertise and ambitious entreprenerus looking to get a step ahead of the competition. Mentor Me has a simple on-boarding process for entrepreneurs and experienced business owners. Those seeking for assistance can create and post a question, edit or delete it later, and search for previously posted questions on Mentor Me.
